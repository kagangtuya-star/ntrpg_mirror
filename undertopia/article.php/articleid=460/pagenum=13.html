异能详述-N、O、P字头系列
 天生护甲(Natural Armor)
　　心灵自塑系(力量)
　　等级：心灵术士4
　　持续时间：1小时/等级 (解消)
　　灵能点：7
　　如同“次级天生护甲”，但你的防御等级得到+4天生防御加值。并且持续时间也更久。
　
Natural Armor
　　Psychometabolism (Str)
　　Level: Psion 4
　　Duration: 1 hour/level (D)
　　Power Points: 7
　　As lesser natural armor, but you gain a +4 natural armor bonus to AC. Also, the duration is considerably longer.
　
消解灵能(Negate Psionics)
　　心灵转化系(体质)
　　等级：心灵术士3
　　异象：视觉
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：中距 (100呎+10呎/等级)
　　目标或区域：单个灵能人物、生物或物品 或 半径30呎的爆发范围
　　持续时间：立即
　　豁免检定：无
　　异能抗力：不可
　　灵能点：5
　　你可以用“消解灵能”来终止展现在生物或物体上的正在生效的异能、暂时压制灵能物品的心灵能力或终止正在区域内生效的异能（至少终止其效果）。被消解的异能如同持续时间结束一样终止。“消解灵能”可以消解（但不能反制）超自然能力的效果，如同消解心灵异能一样。“消解灵能”也能如同影响异能（和法术）一样地消解类法术能力。你不能用“消解灵能”持续时间为“立即”的异能造成的效果。
　　你在“消解灵能”的两种用法间选择其一：目标性消解和区域性消解。
　　目标性消解：本异能的目标是单个物体、生物或异能。你针对该异能或是物体或生物上正在生效的每道异能进行消解检定。消解检定为 1d20 + 显能者等级(至多+10)，对抗的DC为 11 + 欲消解的异能的显能者等级。
　　如果你以灵能物品为目标，你必须针对该物品的显能者等级进行消解检定。如果检定成功，该物品的所有灵能属性被压制1d4轮，之後物品恢复正常。灵能物品在被压制期间变成非灵能物品。
　　针对自己所展现的异能的消解检定将自动成功。
　　区域性解除：本异能影响30呎半径内的所有事物。
　　对每个带有一道或多道异能的生物，你对其所带的显能者等级最高的异能进行消解检定。如果失败，则对显能者等级次高的异能进行检定，以此类推，直到成功消解一道异能（这道“消解灵能”对此目标再无他效）或全部检定都失败。生物所拥有的灵能物品不受影响。
　　对每个带有一道或多道异能的物体，以对生物同样的方式进行消解检定。灵能物品不受区域性消解的影响。
　　对每道有效区域的中心位于“消解灵能”有效区域内的正在生效的异能，你针对该异能进行消解检定以消解之。
　　对每道有效区域和消解区域交迭的异能，你针对该异能进行消解检定，但只能消解交迭区域的效果。
　
Negate Psionics
　　Psychokinesis (Con)
　　Level: Psion 3
　　Display: Vi
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
　　Target or Area: One psionic character or creature, or one object; or 30-ft.-radius burst
　　Duration: Instantaneous
　　Saving Throw: None
　　Power Resistance: No
　　Power Points: 5
　　You can use negate psionics to end ongoing powers that are manifested on a creature or object, to temporarily suppress the psionic abilities of a psionic item, or to end ongoing power (or at least their effects) within an area. A negated power ends as if its duration had expired. Negate psionics can negate (but not counter) the ongoing effects of supernatural abilities as well as psionic powers. Negate psionics affects spelllike abilities just as it affects powers (and spells). You can’t use negate psionics to undo the effects of any power with instantaneous duration.
　　You choose to use negate psionics in one of two ways: a targeted negation or an area negation:
　　Targeted Negation: One object, creature, or power is the target of the power. You make a negation check against the power or against each ongoing power currently in effect on the object or creature. A negation check is 1d20 + 1 per manifester level (maximum +10) against a DC of 11 + the power-to-be-negated’s manifester level.
　　If the object that you target is a psionic item, you make a negation check against the item’s manifester level. If you succeed, all the item’s psionic properties are suppressed for 1d4 rounds, after which the item recovers on its own. A suppressed item becomes nonpsionic for the duration of the effect.
　　You automatically succeed in your negation check against any power that you manifest yourself.
　　Area Negation: The power affects everything within a 30-foot radius.
　　For each creature that is the target of one or more powers, you make a negation check against the power with the highest manifester level. If that fails, you make negation checks against progressively weaker powers until you negate one power (which discharges the negate psionics so far as that target is concerned) or fail all your checks. The creature’s psionic items are not affected.
　　For each object that is the target of one or more powers, you make negation checks as with creatures. Psionic items are not affected by area negations.
　　For each ongoing power with an area centered within the negate psionics target area, you make a negation check to negate the power.
　　For each ongoing power whose area overlaps with that of the negation, you make a negation check to end the effect but only within the area of the negate psionics.
　
回避侦测(Nondetection)
　　心灵预言系(感知)
　　等级：心灵术士3
　　异象：无
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：接触
　　目标：接触到的生物或物体
　　持续时间：1小时/等级
　　豁免检定：意志，通过则无效 (无害、物体)
　　异能抗力：可 (无害、物体)
　　灵能点：5
　　受保护的生物或物体变得难以用诸如“锐耳术/鹰眼术”和“遥视术”这样的心灵预言系异能或者让他人能从远处观察目标的灵能和魔法物品侦测。如果有人尝试对受保护的生物或物品展现心灵预言系异能，则必须通过显能者等级检定(1d20 + 显能者等级)，DC为 11 + 展现“回避侦测”者的显能者等级。如果你是对自己或自己的持有物展现的“回避侦测”，那么该DC为 15 + 你的显能者等级。
　　如果对生物展现该异能，“回避侦测”除了保护该生物本身，还保护该生物的装备。
　
Nondetection
　　Clairsentience (Wis)
　　Level: Psion 3
　　Display: None
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Touch
　　Target: Creature or object touched
　　Duration: 1 hour/level
　　Saving Throw: Will negates (harmless, object)
　　Power Resistance: Yes (harmless, object)
　　Power Points: 5
　　The warded creature or object becomes difficult to detect by Clairsentience powers such as clairaudience/clairvoyance and remote viewing, or psionic and magic items that allow others to view the subject from afar. If a Clairsentience power is attempted against the warded creature or item, the manifester of the Clairsentience power must succeed at a manifester level check (1d20 + manifester level) with a DC of 11 + the manifester level of the psionic creature or character who manifested nondetection. If you manifested nondetection on yourself or on an item currently in your possession, the DC is 15 + your manifester level.
　　If manifested on a creature, nondetection wards the creature’s gear as well as the creature itself.
　
反灵能力场(Null Psionics Field)
　　心灵转化系(体质)
　　等级：心灵术士6
　　异象：视觉、听觉
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：10呎
　　区域：以你为中心，半径10呎的弥漫范围
　　持续时间：10分钟/等级 (解消)
　　豁免检定：无
　　异能抗力：见说明
　　灵能点：11
　　隐形屏障环绕着你并随你移动。屏障内的空间不受大多数灵能效果影响。同样地，其内的任何灵能物品和心灵异能也都失效。该力场对任何在其内使用、带入其内或对其内部使用的异能或灵能效果都有禁制作用，但并不会消解它们。例如，被支配的生物在力场内时不被支配，但只要他离开力场，支配就会重新生效。异能受禁制的时间依然计入其持续时间。
　　进入力场（或在创造力场时位于其内）的星界构装体会消失，直到力场不再覆盖其消失的地点时才在同一地点重新出现。消失期间正常计入制造该生物的异能的持续时间。如果构装体有异能抗力，你在展现“反灵能力场”时必须针对构造体的PR进行显能者等级检定(1d20 + 显能者等级)以使其消失。
　　灵能剑在异能影响区域内灵能效果无效，但仍是一把剑（而且是精制品）。在创造过程中注入灵能，但完成後即自行活动的构装生物（和只在异能持续期间存在的星质构装体不同）则不受本异能影响。亡灵及外位面者也同样不受影响（除非是召唤而来的）。然而这些生物的心灵异能、类法术能力和超自然能力会暂时被力场废除。
　　“消解灵能”无法移除本力场。两个或多个“反灵能力场”彼此并无影响。神器、圣迹(relics)和半神或更高位阶的生物不受此种凡间灵能的影响。
　　注意：若你的体型大于力场覆盖的区域，则超出范围的身体部位不受力场影响。
　
Null Psionics Field
　　Psychokinesis (Con)
　　Level: Psion 6
　　Display: Vi, Au
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: 10 ft.
　　Area: A 10-ft.-radius emanation, centered on you
　　Duration: 10 minutes/level (D)
　　Saving Throw: None
　　Power Resistance: See text
　　Power Points: 11
　　An invisible barrier surrounds you and moves with you. The space within this barrier is totally impervious to most psionic effects. Likewise, it prevents the functioning of any psionic items or psionic powers within its confines. The field suppresses any powers or psionic effects used within, brought into, or manifested into the area, but does not negate them. A dominated creature, for example, is not dominated while inside the field, but the power resumes functioning when it leaves the field. Time spent within a null psionics field counts against the suppressed power’s duration.
　　Astral constructs wink out if they enter the field (or are inside one when it is created). The construct reappears in the same spot once the null psionics field no longer covers the spot where the construct was. Time spent winked out counts normally against the duration of the power that’s maintaining the creature. If the construct has power resistance, when you manifest the null psionics field you must make a manifester level check (1d20 + manifester level) against the construct’s PR to make it wink out.
　　A psionic sword does not function psionically within the area, but it is still a sword (and a masterwork sword at that). The power has no effect on constructs that are imbued with psionics during their creation process and are thereafter selfsupporting (unlike astral constructs that persist only so long as a power’s duration). Undead and outsiders are likewise unaffected (unless summoned). These creatures’ psionic powers, spell-like abilities, or supernatural abilities, however, may be temporarily nullified by the field.
　　A negate psionics power does not remove the field. Two or more null psionics fields have no appreciable effect on each other. Artifacts, relics, and creatures of demigod or higher status are unaffected by mortal psionics such as this.
　　Note: Should you be larger than the area enclosed by the field, any part of your person that lies outside the field is unaffected by the field.
　
橡木身躯(Oak Body)
　　心灵自塑系(力量)
　　等级：心灵术士7
　　异象：听觉
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：个人
　　目标：自己
　　持续时间：1分钟/等级 (解消)
　　灵能点：13
　　如同“钢铁身躯”，但本异能是将你的身体变为活生生的橡木。
　　你得到伤害减免20/+1。你对目盲、属性伤害、耳聋、疾病、溺水、毒素、震慑以及所有影响你的生理系统与呼吸的异能、法术或攻击免疫，因为在本异能持续期间你不具有任何生理构造，也没有呼吸。你只受到3/4的强酸与寒冷伤害，无论其来源。但你也容易被任何能影响木制品的特殊攻击所影响。
　　你的力量得到+3增强加值，但是你的敏捷受到-3增强减值（敏捷值最低为1）。你的速度降为正常的一半。你无法喝水（因此无法使用药水），也不能吹奏管乐器。
　　你的徒手攻击造成1d6点正常伤害（小体型的显能者造成1d4伤害），并且你的徒手攻击被视为“武装”的。
　　你的体重增加为原来的5倍，但是你能漂浮在水面上。
　
Oak Body
　　Psychometabolism (Str)
　　Level: Psion 7
　　Display: Au
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Personal
　　Target: You
　　Duration: 1 minute/level (D)
　　Power Points: 13
　　As iron body, but this power transforms your body into living oak.
　　You gain damage reduction 20/+1. You are immune to blindness, damage to ability scores, deafness, disease, drowning, poison, stunning, and all powers, spells, or attacks that affect your physiology or respiration, because you have no physiology or respiration while this power is in effect. You only suffer three-quarters damage from acid and cold of all kinds. However, you also become vulnerable to all special attacks that affect wood.
　　You gain a +3 enhancement bonus to Strength, but you suffer a -3 enhancement penalty to Dexterity as well (to a minimum score of 1), and your speed is reduced to half normal. You cannot drink (and thus can’t use potions), nor play wind instruments.
　　Your unarmed attacks deal 1d6 points of normal damage, and you are considered “armed” when making unarmed attacks (a Small manifester instead deals 1d4 points of damage).
　　Your weight increases by a factor of five, but you float in water.
　
阅读物体(Object Reading)
　　心灵预言系(感知)
　　等级：心灵术士1
　　异象：听觉、物质
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：接触
　　目标：单一物体
　　持续时间：专注，最长10分钟/等级 (解消)
　　豁免检定：无
　　异能抗力：可
　　灵能点：1
　　你能获悉无生命物体以前的主人的详细资料。物体上积聚着的以前的主人留下的精神痕迹可以被本异能读取。揭示的信息量取决于你对特定物体研究了多久。
　　第一轮：前一任主人的种族。
　　第二轮：前一任主人的性别。
　　第三轮：前一任主人的年龄。
　　第四轮：前一任主人的阵营。
　　第五轮：前一任主人如何得到和失去该物体。
　　第六轮及以後：前前任主人的种族，依此类推。
　　以前没有主人的物品不会揭示出信息。只要异能持续，你可以继续依次追溯前任主人列表并获悉他们的详细资料。如果你再次阅读同样的物体，你不会接着上次在前任主人列表中中断的位置继续。
　
Object Reading
　　Clairsentience (Wis)
　　Level: Psion 1
　　Display: Au, Ma
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Touch
　　Target: One object
　　Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes/level (D)
　　Saving Throw: None
　　Power Resistance: Yes
　　Power Points: 1
　　You can learn details of an inanimate object’s previous owner. Objects accumulate psychic impressions left by their previous owners, which can be read by use of this power. The amount of information revealed depends on how long you study a particular object.
　　1st Round: Last owner’s race.
　　2nd Round: Last owner’s gender.
　　3rd Round: Last owner’s age.
　　4th Round: Last owner’s alignment.
　　5th Round: How last owner gained and lost the object.
　　6th+ Round: Previous-to-last owner’s race, and so on.
　　An object without any previous owners reveals no information. You can continue to run down the list of previous owners and learn details about them as long as the power’s duration lasts. If you read the same object again, you do not pick up where you left off in the list of previous owners.
　
痛苦之触(Painful Touch)
　　心灵自塑系(力量)
　　等级：心灵术士2、心灵武士2
　　异象：视觉、物质
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：个人
　　目标：自己
　　持续时间：1轮/等级 (解消)
　　灵能点：3
　　你的徒手攻击造成额外的伤害。当你成功的进行徒手攻击并造成伤害（或配合“牙”与“爪”系列的异能使用），你对目标造成额外的1d6点淤伤。
　
Painful Touch
　　Psychometabolism (Str)
　　Level: Psion 2/Psychic Warrior 2
　　Display: Vi, Ma
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Personal
　　Target: You
　　Duration: 1 round/level (D)
　　Power Points: 3
　　Your unarmed attacks cause additional pain. When you make a successful unarmed attack that deals damage (or in conjunction with any bite or claw powers), you deal an additional 1d6 points of subdual damage to the target.
　
相位门(Phase Door)
　　心灵传送系(敏捷)
　　等级：心灵术士7
　　异象：视觉
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：接触
　　效果：一个5呎×8呎的以太位面通道，深度1呎/等级
　　持续时间：使用一次/2等级
　　豁免检定：无
　　异能抗力：不可
　　灵能点：13
　　本异能可以在木墙、土墙或石墙上打开一个以太位面通道，对其他材质的墙壁无效。“相位门”是隐形的，而且只有你可以看到并使用它。一旦踏入“相位门”你就会消失，直到从另一端离开才会再度出现。如果你愿意，也可以带一个体型不超过中型的生物同行，但视为使用两次。光线、声音、灵能和法术效果都无法穿过此门。所以你也没法看到门对面的东西。展现“相位门”是个逃跑的好办法，但某些生物（例如相位蜘蛛）还是能轻易的追上你。灵能或魔法“真知术”效果可以揭示 “相位门”，但他人还是无法使用此门。
　　“消解灵能”可以解除已经存在的“相位门”。如果此时有生物正在门内，该生物会由最近的出口弹出，就如同在魔法通道内那样。
　　展现“异能恒定术”可以使一个“相位门”永远存在。你可以在门上设定一些触发条件，使别人也能使用此门。这些条件可以很简单也可以很繁杂。你可以设定有形特质为触发条件，如名字、身份或阵营等，但不能用等级、职业、HD或生命值等无形特质作为条件。
　
Phase Door
　　Psychoportation (Dex)
　　Level: Psion 7
　　Display: Vi
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Touch
　　Effect: Ethereal 5-ft. x 8-ft. opening, 1 ft./level deep
　　Duration: One usage/2 levels
　　Saving Throw: None
　　Power Resistance: No
　　Power Points: 13
　　You create an ethereal passage through wooden, plaster, or stone walls, but not other materials. The phase door is invisible and inaccessible to all creatures except you, and only you can use the passage. You disappear when you enter the phase door and appear when you exit. If you desire, you can take one other creature (Medium-size or smaller) through the door. This counts as two uses of the door. The door does not allow light, sound, psionic effects, or spell effects through it, nor can you see through it without using it. Thus, the power can provide an escape route, though certain creatures, such as phase spiders, can follow with ease. True seeing psionics or magical effects reveal a phase door but do not allow its use.
　　A phase door is subject to negate psionics. If anyone is within the passage when it is negated, she is harmlessly ejected just as if she were inside a magical passwall effect.
　　A phase door can be made permanent with a incarnate power. You can allow other creatures to use the phase door by setting some triggering condition for the door. Such conditions can be as simple or elaborate as you desire. They can be based on a creature’s name, identity, or alignment, but otherwise must be based on observable actions or qualities. Intangibles such as level, class, HD, and hit points don’t qualify.
　
位面传送(Plane Shift)
　　心灵传送系(敏捷)
　　等级：心灵术士7
　　异象：听觉、精神

　　你可以指定新形态的身体特征（比如毛色、高度和性别），但不能超出该生物应有的正常范围。你能够轻易的易容为该种族的一员。如果你展现本异能来进行易容，则“易容”检定可以得到+10加值。

Any part of the body or piece of equipment that is separated from the whole reverts to its original form.
　　You choose your form's physical qualities (such as hair color, height, and gender), but they must fall within the normal ranges for the form. You are effectively disguised as an average member of the new form's race. If you use this power to create a disguise, you get a +10 bonus on your Disguise check.

预知术(Precognition)
　　心灵预言系(感知)
　　等级：心灵术士6
　　异象：视觉、听觉
　　显能时间：10分钟
　　距离：个人
　　目标：自己
　　持续时间：1分钟/等级 (解消)
　　灵能点：11
　　类似“预言术”但更强，“预知术”异能可对将来一年内发生的关于一个特定目标、事件或行动的问题提供有用的景象。景象可能是有意义的图像的简短闪现，也可能是持续数分钟的无删节的梦景形式。
　　例如，假设问题是“下面这一年里我等面对的最大危险是什么？”DM最终决定让PC们对抗一条他准备了多时的黑龙，并且他认为这最可能成为队伍“命中注定”的最大威胁。因此“预知术”的回应可能是这样的景象：“你看见你和同伴们站在高大的、黑暗的洞口前。洞口被散发着蒸汽的强酸池挡住了，而周围的石头尽被强酸烧焦，并且有着只有巨大的爪子才能造成的撕裂。”无论如何，DM控制着你得到的信息。注意，如果你的队伍没有根据情报行动，条件的改变可能导致情报变得无用。
　　正确预知的基本几率是80%加每显能者等级1%。若环境需要DM可调整该几率（例如，针对心灵预言系异能的特别防范的存在）。如果掷骰失败，你将知道异能失败了，除非制造虚假信息的特殊灵能或魔法正在生效。
　　与“预言术”相同，同一显能者对同一问题多次使用“预知术”，都将得到和第一次同样的回答。
　
Precognition
　　Clairsentience (Wis)
　　Level: Psion 6
　　Display: Vi, Au
　　Manifestation Time: 10 minutes
　　Range: Personal
　　Target: You
　　Duration: 1 minute/level (D)
　　Power Points: 11
　　Similar to divination but more powerful, the precognition power provides you with a useful vision in reply to a question concerning a specific goal, event, or activity that is to occur within a year. The vision can be as simple as a short glimpse of a meaningful image, or it might take the form of a fulllength dream lasting several minutes.
　　 For example, suppose the question is “What is the greatest danger our party will face in the coming year?” The DM eventually wants to pit the player characters against a black dragon he has long prepared, and he considers this is in all likelihood the worst threat that “fate” has in store for the party. Therefore the precognition response might be this vision: “You see you and your comrades standing before the mouth of a high, dark cave-mouth. The entrance is blocked by a steaming pool of acid, while the rock all around is scorched by acid and lacerated by what can only be gargantuan claws.” In all cases, the DM controls what information you receive. Note that if your party doesn’t act on the information, the conditions may change so that the information is no longer useful.
　　The base chance for a correct precognition is 80%, plus 1% per manifester level. The DM adjusts the chance if unusual circumstances require it (if, for example, unusual precautions against Clairsentience powers have been taken). If the dice roll fails, you know the power failed, unless specific psionics or magic yielding false information is at work.
　　As with divinations, multiple precognitions about the same action by the same manifester use the same die roll result as the first precognition and yield the same answer each time.

　　你可以修补精神损伤。你也可以授予其他生物你所知道的异能。
　　修补损伤：你可以移除任何强迫与魅惑（比如“呼吸危机”和“厌世术”），甚至那些高于六级的（比如“真实支配术”和“奴役术”）。事实上，你可以利用“心灵手术”移除任何由心灵异能造成的正在持续的或永久的效果，除了生命值的损伤以外。
　　你也可以恢复受术者所有被吸取的等级（或者解除所有负向等级），无论他是怎样失去这些等级的，受术者将被恢复至他以前达到过的最高等级。这些被吸取的等级只有在失去後不超过每显能者等级一小时的时间内才能被恢复。与“改造灵光”不同，这些效果在异能展现完毕後立即停止或被解除，不需要再进行豁免检定。你也可以解除所有伤害受术者属性的异能效果，治愈所有的暂时属性伤害并且恢复所有的永久属性吸取。“心灵手术”同样移除所有形式的精神错乱、困惑以及类似效果，但是它不能恢复因死亡而丧失的等级或体质值。
　　传授知识：如果愿意，你可以直接把你所知的一个异能传授给另一个灵能人物。你无法传授比受术者所能展现的异能更高级的异能。但是你可以传授本来不在此人物的异能列表中的异能。通过“心灵手术”传授的异能不计入人物每等级可知的异能数。
　　经验值费用：每次向另一生物传授一道异能，你需要支付的经验值等于 3000×所传授异能的能级。
　
Psychic Chirurgery
　　Telepathy (Cha)
　　Level: Psion 9
　　Display: Vi, Au, Me
　　Manifestation Time: 10 minutes
　　Range: Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
　　Target: One living creature
　　Duration: Instantaneous
　　Saving Throw: Will negates
　　Power Resistance: Yes
　　Power Points: 17, XP cost (see text)
　　You can repair psychic damage. You can also grant another creature knowledge of powers you know.
　　Repair Damage: You can remove any compulsions ant charms (such as crisis of breath and fatal attraction), even those higher than 6th level (such as true domination and thrall). In fact, you can remove any ongoing or permanent effect caused by a psychic power with psychic chirurgery, save for hit points.
　　You can also restore all drained levels (or disperse all negative levels) afflicting the subject, regardless of how he lost those levels, restoring him to the highest level he had previously achieved. The drained levels are restored only if the time since the subject lost the level is no more than 1 hour per manifester level. Unlike aura alteration, these effects end or are reversed as soon as the manifestation time is completed without need for another saving throw. You can also remove all psionic effects penalizing the subject’s abilities, heal all temporary damage to all ability _ and restore all points permanently drained from all ability scores. Psychic chirurgery also removes all forms of insanity, confusion, and so on, but it does not restore levels or Constitution points lost due to death.
　　Transfer Knowledge: If desired, you can directly transfer knowledge of a power you know to another psionic character. You can’t give a character knowledge of a psionic power of a level higher than she could normally manifest, but you could give her knowledge of a power that is not normally on the character’s power list. Knowledge of powers granted through psychic chirurgery does not count toward the maximum number of powers a character can know per level.
　　XP Cost: Each time you use psychic chirurgery to implant knowledge of a power in another creature, you pay an XP cost equal to 3,000 x the level of the power implanted.
　
心力吸取(Psychic Vampire)
　　心灵转化系(体质)
　　等级：心灵术士5、心灵武士5
　　异象：视觉 (见说明)、精神
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：接触
　　目标：接触到的生物
　　持续时间：1轮/等级
　　豁免检定：强韧，通过则无效
　　异能抗力：可
　　灵能点：9
　　本异能用可以吸取对手灵能的黑暗裹住你的手。成功的近战接触攻击将从对手处吸取2灵能点/等级并立即转化为你的。你无法获得超过每日可用点数的灵能点。吸来的灵能点始终保留在你的馀量中，直到被正常用掉。附着异能的接触攻击被视为武装攻击。
　　对抗没有灵能点的灵能生物（例如灵能点馀量为空的灵能人物或是免费显能的灵能怪物）时，你的接触攻击改为造成1点暂时智力、感知或魅力伤害（由你选择）。你从这次吸取中获得1点灵能点。对于非灵能对手也是同样的效果。
　
Psychic Vampire
　　Psychokinesis (Con)
　　Level: Psion 5/Psychic Warrior 5
　　Display: Vi (see text), Me
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Touch
　　Target: Creature touched
　　Duration: 1 round/level
　　Saving Throw: Fortitude negates
　　Power Resistance: Yes
　　Power Points: 9
　　This power shrouds your hand with darkness that you can use to drain an opponent’s power. Your successful melee touch attack drains 2 power points/level from your foe and transfers them immediately to you. You can’t gain more power points than you normally have available in a day. The stolen power points remain in your reserve until used normally. Your touch attack, charged with a psionic power, is armed as an armed attack.
　　Against psionic beings who have no power points (such as a psionic character with an empty power point reserve, or a psionic monster that freely manifests its powers), your touch instead deals 1 point of temporary Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma damage (your choice). You gain 1 power point from this drain. You have the same effect on nonpsionic opponents.
　
心力回馈(Psychofeedback)
　　心灵自塑系(力量)
　　等级：心灵术士4、心灵武士4
　　异象：视觉
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：个人
　　目标：自己
　　持续时间：1分钟 (解消)
　　灵能点：7
　　你能够利用灵能点来增强你的力量、敏捷和体质调整值，这是即时动作。在异能持续期间，你每轮都能使用灵能点来增强你本轮内的属性调整值（并不是属性值），增强的数量等于你在该轮花费的灵能点数量的一半。比如，你可以将你的力量调整值增加8点（如果你花费16点灵能点）。除非你在下一轮内又花费16 点，否则你的力量修正值将恢复到原有的较低水平。只要你花费所需要的灵能点费用的总和，你也可以同时改变两项属性值，或者同时改变全部三项。你并不需要持续的增强任何属性调整值来维持本异能——只要在异能持续时间内，你就能够在任何回合增强属性调整值。
　
Psychofeedback
　　Psychometabolism (Str)
　　Level: Psion 4/Psychic Warrior 4
　　Display: Vi
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Personal
　　Target: You
　　Duration: 1 minute (D)
　　Power Points: 7
　　You can use power points to boost your Strength, Dexterity, and Constitution modifiers as a free action. While the duration lasts, you can use power points on a round-by-round basis to boost any or all of your ability score modifiers (not the actual ability score) by a number equal to half the power points you expend for that round as a free action. For example, you can boost your Strength modifier by as much as 8 points (if you spend 16 power points). Unless you again spend 16 power points the following round, though, your Strength modifier returns to its former lower level. You could simultaneously boost two scores, or all three, as long as you pay the total power point cost. You don’t have to boost an ability modifier every round to keep the power in effect—as long as the duration lasts, you have the potential to boost your ability modifiers on any round you choose.
　
银光术(Psycholuminescence)
　　心灵创造系(智力)
　　等级：心灵术士1
　　异象：视觉 (见说明)、听觉
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：接触
　　目标：接触到的物体
　　持续时间：10分钟/等级
　　豁免检定：无
　　异能抗力：不可
　　灵能点：1
　　被碰触的物体发出银色的光，散布于20呎半径内。在强光下受到减值的生物若暴露在银光中将受到同样的减值。本异能无法被展现在生物上。如果本异能被展现于一个小物品上，而该物品被挡光的东西完全遮住，则本异能的效果被阻碍，直到遮盖物被移开为止。
　　被带入魔法黑暗的银光（反之亦然）将暂时的被抵消，所以两者效果重叠的区域将保持原有的光亮度（除非使用变体规则“灵能异于魔法”）。
　
Psycholuminescence
　　Metacreativity (Int)
　　Level: Psion 1
　　Display: Vi (see text), Au
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Touch
　　Target: Object touched
　　Duration: 10 minutes/level
　　Saving Throw: None
　　Power Resistance: No
　　Power Points: 1
　　The object touched begins to glow with a silvery light, shedding in a 20-foot radius. Creatures who suffer penalties in bright light suffer them while exposed to this psycholuminescence. The power cannot be manifested on a creature. If the power is manifested on a small object that is then inside or under a lightproof covering, the power’s effects are blocked until the covering is removed.
　　Psycholuminescence brought into an area of magical darkness (or vice versa) is temporarily negated, so that the orherwise prevailing light conditions exist in the overlapping areas (unless the Psionics Are Different option is in force).
