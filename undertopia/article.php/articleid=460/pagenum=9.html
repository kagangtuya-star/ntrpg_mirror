异能详述-F字头系列
 
鬼斧神工(Fabricate)
　　心灵创造系(智力)
　　等级：心灵术士4
　　异象：物质
　　显能时间：见说明
　　距离：近距 (25呎+5呎/2等级)
　　目标：至多1立方码/等级 (见说明)
　　持续时间：立即
　　豁免检定：无
　　异能抗力：不可
　　灵能点：7
　　可将某种原料转变为同种原料的物品。例如将一丛树木转变为一座木桥，一片荨麻转变为一条麻绳，亚麻布或羊毛转变为衣服等。生物以及灵能物品无法被“鬼斧神工”创造或改变。转变出来的物品品质与其原料品质相当，如果作用对象是矿物，目标大小的限制减为“至多每等级1立方呎”而非“每等级1立方码”。
　　若要制造需要熟练工匠技术才能制做的物品（例如珠宝，剑，玻璃制品，水晶制品等），你必须通过相关的“手艺”技能检定。
　　本异能每作用于1立方码（或1立方呎）原料就需要1整轮显能时间。
　
Fabricate
　　Metacreativity (Int)
　　Level: Psion 4
　　Display: Ma
　　Manifestation Time: See text
　　Range: Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
　　Target: Up to 1 cubic yard/level (see text)
　　Duration: Instantaneous
　　Saving Throw: None
　　Power Resistance: No
　　Power Points: 7
　　You convert material of one sort into a product that is of the same material. Thus, you can fabricate a wooden bridge from a clump of trees, a rope from a patch of hemp, clothes from flax or wool, and so forth. Creatures and psionic or magic items cannot be created or altered by fabricate. The quality of items made by this power is commensurate with the quality of material used as the basis for the new fabrication. If you work with a mineral, the target is reduced to 1 cubic foot per manifester level instead of 1 cubic yard.
　　You must make an appropriate Craft check to fabricate articles requiring a high degree of craftsmanship (jewelry, swords, glass, crystal, and so on).
　　Manifesting requires 1 full round per cubic yard (or cubic foot) of material to be affected by the power.
　
伪造感觉(False Sensory Input)
　　心灵附魔系(魅力)[影响心灵]
　　等级：心灵术士3
　　异象：精神
　　显能时间：一整轮
　　距离：远距 (400呎+40呎/等级)
　　目标：单一活物
　　持续时间：专注，最长1分钟/等级 (解消)
　　豁免检定：意志，通过则无效
　　异能抗力：可
　　灵能点：5
　　你能够有限的伪造受术者的一种感觉。受术者以为自己看到、听到、闻到、嗅到或摸到的是与真实情况不同的事物。你不能凭空捏造一个感觉，也不能使受术者完全忽略一个感觉，但是你可以将一种感觉伪造成不同的感觉。例如，你可以令一个人类看起来像是个矮人（或者使一个人看上去像是另一个人）、令一扇关着的门看上去像是开着的、令一缸酸液闻起来就像玫瑰香水、令一只鹦鹉看上去像是镇纸、令腐烂的食物尝起来像是新鲜的水果、令轻推感觉上就像匕首的戳刺、令尖叫听上去像是风的呼啸等等。你每轮都可以选择你所伪造的感觉。你最多能把一个感觉的“强度”改变50%。因此，你不能让一个城堡看上去像是个茅屋，但你可以让它像另一个城堡，或一个与其体积类似的不规则的小丘。虽然你可以让酸雾闻上去很舒服，但你不可能让它像糖一样甜。如果本异能被用来使对方正在显能的的心灵术士分神，对手必须通过“专注”检定，如同对抗一个非伤害性异能（这种情况下DC为被分神的异能的DC+3）。
　
False Sensory Input
　　Telepathy (Cha) [Mind-Affecting]
　　Level: Psion 3
　　Display: Me
　　Manifestation Time: 1 full round
　　Range: Long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level)
　　Target: One living creature.
　　Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute/level (D)
　　Saving Throw: Will negates
　　Power Resistance: Yes
　　Power Points: 5
　　You have a limited ability to falsify one of the subject’s senses. The subject thinks she sees, hears, smells, tastes or feels something other than what her senses actually report. You can’t fabricate a sensation where none exists, nor make the subject completely oblivious to a sensation, but you can falsify the specifics of one sensation for different specifics. For instance, you could make a human look like a dwarf (or one human look like another specific human), a closed door look like it is open, a vat of acid smell like rose water, a parrot look like a bookend, stale rations taste like fresh fruit, a light pat feel like a dagger thrust, a scream sound like the howling wind, and so on. You can switch between senses you falsify round by round. You can’t alter a sensation’s “intensity” by more than 50%. Thus, you couldn’t make a castle look like a hovel, but you could make it look like a different castle, or a rough hillock of approximately the same size. While you might be able to make acidic fumes smell nice, you can’t get acid to taste like candy. If this power is used to distract an enemy psion who is attempting to manifest a power, the enemy must make a Concentration check as if against a nondamaging power (the DC equals the distracting power’s save DC + 3 in this case).
　
隔空之手(Far Hand)
　　心灵转化系(体质)
　　等级：心灵术士0
　　异象：视觉
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：近距 (25呎+5呎/2等级)
　　目标：重量不超过5磅的无人持用的非魔法物品
　　持续时间：专注
　　豁免检定：无
　　异能抗力：不可
　　灵能点：1
　　你可以从远处随意提起并移动物品。你可以以移动等效动作向任何方向移动该物体15呎。如果你和物体间的距离超过了异能有效距离，本异能就终止了。
　
Far Hand
　　Psychokinesis (Con)
　　Level: Psion 0
　　Display: Vi
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
　　Target: A nonmagical, unattended object weighing up to 5 pounds
　　Duration: Concentration
　　Saving Throw: None
　　Power Resistance: No
　　Power Points: 1
　　You can lift and move an object at will from a distance. As a move-equivalent action, you can move the object up to 15 feet in any direction, though the power ends if the distance between you and the object ever exceeds the power’s range.
　
隔空之击(Far Punch)
　　心灵转化系(体质)
　　等级：心灵术士0、心灵武士0
　　异象：视觉、精神
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：近距 (25呎+5呎/2等级)
　　目标：单个生物或个体
　　持续时间：立即
　　豁免检定：无
　　异能抗力：可
　　灵能点：1
　　你选择的单个目标被念动力打击并造成1点伤害。“格空之击”总能命中你看得见的目标，即使目标正在缠斗或有部分掩蔽或隐蔽。然而，“护盾术”可以保护目标。本异能无法伤害死物（门、墙、锁等等）。
　
Far Punch
　　Psychokinesis (Con)
　　Level: Psion 0/Psychic Warrior 0
　　Display: Vi, Me
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
　　Target: One individual
　　Duration: Instantaneous
　　Saving Throw: None
　　Power Resistance: Yes
　　Power Points: 1
　　A target you select is buffeted with telekinetic force for 1 point of damage.
　　Far punch always affects a target within range that you can see, even if the target is in melee or has partial cover or concealment. However, a shield spell protects the target. Inanimate objects (doors, walls, locks, and so on) cannot be damaged by the power.
　
厌世术(Fatal Attraction)
　　心灵附魔系(魅力)[胁迫、影响心灵]
　　等级：心灵术士4
　　异象：精神 (只有受术者可感知)
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：中距 (100呎+10呎/等级)
　　目标：单一活物
　　持续时间：立即
　　豁免检定：强韧，通过则无效
　　异能抗力：可
　　灵能点：7
　　你向受术者的潜意识植入一种潜藏的对死亡的强烈欲望。这种欲望会慢慢的生根并在受术者心灵的某个角落渐渐成长1d4天时间。受术者仍然对心中如溃疡般悄悄生长的死亡欲毫无察觉。如果受术者的伙伴们（如果有）在此阶段的最後一天通过感知检定，则会发现受术者显得莫名其妙的阴沉和听天由命。
　　当这种欲望增长为无法抗拒的变态心理时（1d4天後），受术者将会寻找最快且最可行的结束自己的生命的手段并试图这样做。比如，如果站在悬崖边，他将会跳崖；如果找不到更好的办法，受术者会对自己进行致命一击；如果他没有武器，他会试图在一面墙或者其他表面上撞死；如果受术者尝试了一种自杀方式而没有成功，他可以再进行一次强韧豁免检定对抗同样的DC来破除这种暗示。若未通过，这种欲望将会再次慢慢增长1d4天，如此继续循环。
　
Fatal Attraction
　　Telepathy (Cha) [Compulsion, Mind-Affecting]
　　Level: Psion 4
　　Display: Me (detectable only by victim)
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
　　Target: One living creature
　　Duration: Instantaneous
　　Saving Throw: Fortitude negates
　　Power Resistance: Yes
　　Power Points: 7
　　You plant a hidden death-urge impulse in the subject’s unconscious. The impulse slowly takes root and reinforces itself in the dark cellars of the subject’s mind over a period of 1d4 days. The subject’s conscious mind remains completely unaware of the death urge secretly swelling within like an abscess. The subject’s companions, if any, may each make, one Wisdom check on the final day of the time period to notice that he seems unaccountably dour and fatalistic.
　　When the urge has grown to an overpowering psychosis (after 1d4 days), the subject looks for the quickest, most likely method to end his life and attempts to do so. For instance, if standing next to a cliff, he would step off. If nothing better offered itself, the subject would attempt to perform a coup de grace on himself. If he had no weapon, he would attempt to batter himself to death on a wall or other surface. If the subject goes through with a method but fails to die, he can make another Fortitude save against the original DC to break the compulsion. Otherwise, the urge secretly grows again over 1d4 days, and the cycle repeats.
　
命运链接(Fate Link)
　　心灵附魔系(魅力)
　　等级：心灵术士3
　　异象：嗅觉
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：近距 (25呎+5呎/2等级)
　　目标：任何两个开始时相距不超过30呎的活物
　　持续时间：1小时/等级
　　豁免检定：意志，通过则无效
　　异能抗力：可
　　灵能点：5
　　如果两个受术生物都没有通过豁免检定，你便能够把他们的命运暂时的连接起来。若这两个生物中的任何一个感到疼痛，则两个都会感觉到。若一个失去生命值，另外一个也失去相同的数量。如果其中一个死亡，另一个必须立刻成功的通过强韧豁免检定，未通过则会得到与死者等级相同的负向等级（最多被降至1级）。其他效果不会经由“命运链接”转移。如果愿意，你也可以创造一条连接了多个生物的命运链。
　
Fate Link
　　Telepathy (Cha)
　　Level: Psion 3
　　Display: Ol
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
　　Target: Any two living creatures that are initially no more than 30 ft. apart.
　　Duration: 1 hour/level
　　Saving Throw: Will negates
　　Power Resistance: Yes
　　Power Points: 5
　　You temporarily link the fates of any two creatures, if both fail their saving throws. If either linked creature experiences pain, both feel it. When one loses hit points, the other loses the same amount. If either dies, the other must immediately succeed at a Fortitude save or gain a number of negative levels equal to the character level of the creature that died (minimum one). All other effects are not transferred by the fate link. You could attempt to create a chain of fate linked creatures, if desired.
　
逆天术(Fate of One)
　　心灵预言系(感知)
　　等级：心灵术士4
　　异象：精神、视觉
　　显能时间：见说明
　　距离：个人
　　目标：自己
　　持续时间：立即
　　灵能点：7
　　你有限的全知能力让你可以重掷豁免检定、攻击检定或技能检定并使用两次掷骰结果中较好的一个。
　　你可以即时展现本异能，快到足够紧要关头带来好处。展现本异能是即时动作，如同展现瞬发异能，这将计入每轮可使用的瞬发异能限制。
　
Fate of One
　　Clairsentience (Wis)
　　Level: Psion 4
　　Display: Me, Vi
　　Manifestation Time: See text
　　Range: Personal
　　Target: You
　　Duration: Instantaneous
　　Power Points: 7
　　Your limited omniscience allows you to reroll a saving throw, attack roll, or skill check, and use the better of the two rolls for your result.
　　You can manifest this power instantly, quickly enough to gain its benefits in a clutch situation. Manifesting the power is a free action, like manifesting a quickened power, and it counts toward the normal limit of one quickened power per round.
　
羽落术(Feather Fall)
　　心灵传送系(敏捷)
　　等级：心灵术士1
　　异象：听觉
　　显能时间：见说明
　　距离：近距 (25呎+5呎/2等级)
　　目标群：半径10呎内的自由落下的总重不超过300磅/等级的物体或生物
　　持续时间：直到落地，或经过1轮/等级
　　豁免检定：意志，通过则无效 (物体)
　　异能抗力：可 (物体)
　　灵能点：1
　　受影响的生物或物体会在空中缓慢落下，落下的速度立即变为每轮约60呎（与从数呎落下的速度相若）。在异能持续时间内落地不会受伤，超过持续时间後就恢复正常下落的速度。
　　你可以即时展现本异能，快到足够在失足跌落时挽救你自己。展现本异能是即时动作，如同展现瞬发异能，这将计入每轮可使用的瞬发异能限制。
　　本异能对远程武器没有影响，除非它是从高空坠落。如果异能目标是从高出正在坠落的物品，例如从城堡上掷下的大石，物品只对下面的生物造成一半伤害（每400磅造成1d6伤害），且没有坠落高度加值。
　　本异能只对自由下落的物体起效。它不能影响剑的挥砍或飞行生物的冲锋。
　
Feather Fall
　　Psychoportation (Dex)
　　Level: Psion 1
　　Display: Au
　　Manifestation Time: See text
　　Range: Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
　　Targets: Any free-falling objects or creatures in a 10-ft. radius whose weight does not total more than 300 lb./level
　　Duration: Until landing or 1 round/level
　　Saving Throw: Will negates (object)
　　Power Resistance: Yes (object)
　　Power Points: 1
　　The creatures or objects affected fall slowly. The rate of fall is instantly changed to a mere 60 feet per round (equivalent to the end of a fall from a few feet), with no damage incurred upon landing while the power is in effect. However, when the duration expires, a normal rate of fall occurs.
　　You can manifest this power instantly, quickly enough to save yourself if you unexpectedly fall. Manifesting the power is a free action, like manifesting a quickened power, and it counts toward the normal limit of one quickened power per round.
　　This power has no special effect on ranged weapons unless they are falling quite a distance. If manifested on a falling item, such as a boulder dropped from the top of a castle wall, the item does half normal damage based on weight (1d6 per 400 pounds) with no bonus for the height of the drop.
　　The power works only upon free-falling objects. It does not affect a sword blow or a charging or flying creature.
　
感光术(Feel Light)
　　心灵自塑系(力量)
　　等级：心灵术士1、心灵武士1
　　异象：物质
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：个人
　　目标：自己
　　持续时间：10分钟/等级 (解消)
　　灵能点：1
　　你能够透过触觉（触到）感受光。你的整个身体都变为视觉器官。换句话说，你的身体代替了你的眼睛。你能“看见”通常你的眼睛能够看见的事物。你的视野范围没有变化，但如果你有黑暗视觉，“感光术”不会把此视觉转变为触觉。
　　如果你的视力正常，扩展後的视觉给予你的“搜索”和“侦察”技能+1增强加值。如果本异能与“听光术”共用，则增强加值为+2。
　
Feel Light
　　Psychometabolism (Str)
　　Level: Psion 1/Psychic Warrior 1
　　Display: Ma
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Personal
　　Target: You
　　Duration: 10 minutes/level (D)
　　Power Points: 1
　　You experience light translated through tactile sensation (by touch). Your entire body becomes a receiver for light. In effect, your body replaces your eyes. You can “see” what your eyes would normally reveal. Your field of vision does not change, but if you have darkvision, sight is not translated to touch via feel light.
　　If your eyes are working normally, the expanded view gives you a +1 enhancement bonus on all Spot and Search checks. If this power is used in conjunction with hear light, the bonus is +2.
　
感声术(Feel Sound)
　　心灵自塑系(力量)
　　等级：心灵术士1、心灵武士1
　　异象：物质
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：个人
　　目标：自己
　　持续时间：10分钟/等级 (解消)
　　灵能点：1
　　如同“感光术”，但你透过触觉（触到）听到声音。你的整个身体都变为听觉器官。换句话说，你的身体代替了你的耳朵。你能“听见”通常你的耳朵能够听见的事物。你的听力范围没有变化。
　　如果你的耳朵正常，扩展後的听觉给予你的“聆听”技能+1增强加值。如果本异能与“观声术”共用，则增强加值为+2。
　
Feel Sound
　　Psychometabolism (Str)
　　Level: Psion 1/Psychic Warrior 1
　　Display: Ma
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Personal
　　Target: You
　　Duration: 10 minutes/level (D)
　　Power Points: 1
　　As feel light, except you experience sound translated through tactile sensation (by touch). Your entire body becomes a receiver for sound. In effect, your body replaces your ears. You can “hear” what your ears would normally reveal. Your range of hearing does not change.
　　If your ears are working normally, the expanded reception gives you a +1 enhancement bonus on all Listen checks. If this power is used in conjunction with see sound, the bonus is +2.
　
燃烧一指(Finger of Fire)
　　心灵创造系(智力)[火焰]
　　等级：心灵术士0
　　异象：视觉 (见说明)
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：近距 (25呎+5呎/2等级)
　　效果：射线
　　持续时间：立即
　　豁免检定：无
　　异能抗力：可
　　灵能点：1
　　一道不稳定的燃着的星质射线从你的指尖射出，你必须通过远程接触攻击检定来使射线对目标造成伤害。此射线造成1d3点火焰伤害。
　
Finger of Fire
　　Metacreativity (Int) [Fire]
　　Level: Psion 0
　　Display: Vi (see text)
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
　　Effect: Ray
　　Duration: Instantaneous
　　Saving Throw: None
　　Power Resistance: Yes
　　Power Points: 1
　　A ray of unstable, burning ectoplasm projects from your pointing finger. You must succeed at a ranged touch attack with the ray to deal damage to a target. The ray deals 1d3 points of fire damage.
　
落炎术(Firefall)
　　心灵创造系(智力)[火焰]
　　等级：心灵术士1
　　异象：视觉 (见说明)
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：20呎
　　区域：半径10呎的爆发范围
　　持续时间：立即
　　豁免检定：反射，通过则效果减半
　　异能抗力：可
　　灵能点：1
　　不稳定的星质微粒忽然爆炸，在你指定的范围内爆开。在此范围内的所有生物都受到1d4点火焰伤害。易燃材料如布料，纸张，羊皮纸，薄木板等等，在被火焰接触到的时候会燃着。人物可以用一个全回合动作熄灭燃烧物上的火。
　
Firefall
　　Metacreativity (Int) [Fire]
　　Level: Psion 1
　　Display: Vi (see text)
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: 20 ft.
　　Area: 10-ft. radius burst
　　Duration: Instantaneous
　　Saving Throw: Reflex half
　　Power Resistance: Yes
　　Power Points: 1
　　Motes of unstable ectoplasm flare and dissipate explosively within the area you designate. Any creature in the area takes 1d4 points of fire damage. Flammable materials such as cloth, paper, parchment, thin wood, and so on, burn if the flames touch them. A character can extinguish burning items as a full-round action.
　
分身术(Fission)
　　心灵自塑系(力量)
　　等级：心灵术士7
　　异象：嗅觉
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：个人
　　目标：自己
　　持续时间：1轮/等级 (解消)
　　灵能点：13
　　你能够把自己分开，在5呎距离内创造一个副本。你副本的思维和举止和你完全一样，并听从你的命令，尽管它不会去做你不愿自己去做的事情。你的副本具有你的属性但没有任何装备。你的副本拥有与你相同的生命值，你一半（扣除本异能的费用之後再行计算）的灵能点数，以及你在展现此异能时所有的其他的身体特征。异能、法术或其他影响你的效果并不影响到你的副本。
　　当异能效果结束或你解除这个异能，你与你的副本重新聚合，无论你们相距多远。聚合时你受到你的副本所受伤害的一半。这有可能把你的生命值降至0或者更低，但这不会把你的生命值降至-10或更少。同样，你失去你的副本所用去的灵能点数的一半。如果其中一个你在异能结束前死亡，那么将不发生聚合；然而你（无论是本体还是副本）获得一个负向等级。
　
Fission
　　Psychometabolism (Str)
　　Level: Psion 7
　　Display: Ol
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Personal
　　Target: You
　　Duration: 1 round/level (D)
　　Power Points: 13
　　You can divide yourself, creating a duplicate up to 5 feet away. Your duplicate thinks and acts exactly as you do and follows your orders, although it will not do anything you wouldn’t do yourself. Your duplicate has all your abilities but none of your equipment. Your duplicate has all your hit points, half your power points (figured after you deduct the cost of this power), and other physical traits you had at the time you manifested the power. Powers, spells, or other effects affecting you do not affect your duplicate.
　　When the duration expires or when you dismiss the power, you and your duplicate rejoin, no matter how far from each other you are. You take half of the damage your duplicate has suffered at the time of rejoining. This could potentially put you at 0 hit points or lower, but it can’t bring you to -10 hit points or less. Also, you lose half of the power points your duplicate used up. If one of you dies before the duration expires, there is no rejoining; however, you (either in your original flesh or that of your duplicate) gain one negative level.
　
烈焰帷幕(Flaming Shroud)
　　心灵创造系(智力)
　　等级：心灵术士6
　　异象：物质 (见说明)
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：中距 (100呎+10呎/等级)
　　目标：一个体型不超过大型的生物
　　持续时间：立即
　　豁免检定：反射，通过则无效
　　异能抗力：可
　　灵能点：11
　　你从星界抽出扭动的丝把受术者绑缚在地狱般的火幕中。如果目标未通过敏捷豁免检定，则会受到11d6点火焰伤害。
　
Flaming Shroud
　　Metacreativity (Int)
　　Level: Psion 6
　　Display: Ma (see text)
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
　　Target: One creature of Large size or smaller
　　Duration: Instantaneous
　　Saving Throw: Reflex negates
　　Power Resistance: Yes
　　Power Points: 11
　　You draw writhing strands of unstable ectoplasm from the Astral Plane and wrap the subject in a shroud of hellish fire. If the target fails its Reflex save, it suffers 11d6 points of fire damage.
　
漂浮术(Float)
　　心灵传送系(敏捷)
　　等级：心灵术士0、心灵武士0
　　异象：听觉
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：近距 (25呎+5呎/2等级)
　　目标群：任何重量不超过300磅/等级的物体或生物
　　持续时间：专注
　　豁免检定：无
　　异能抗力：不可
　　灵能点：1
　　你可以用精神力支撑一个在附近的水或类似液体中的生物或物体。你无法移动漂浮物，但如果本异能的受术者是活物，他可以不用担心沉没（或溺死，如果受术者不擅长游泳）地在水中移动。一旦受术者与显能者的距离超过异能有效距离，则本异能结束。
　
Float
　　Psychoportation (Dex)
　　Level: Psion 0/Psychic Warrior 0
　　Display: Au
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
　　Targets: Any object or creature whose weight does not total more than 300 lb./level
　　Duration: Concentration
　　Saving Throw: None
　　Power Resistance: No
　　Power Points: 1
　　You can mentally support one creature or object in water or similar liquid at will from a distance. You cannot move the floating object, but if the subject of the power is animate, it can move through the water without fear of submerging (or drowning, if an unskilled swimmer). The power ends if the distance between you and the subject ever exceeds the power’s range.
　
飞行术(Fly)
　　心灵传送系(敏捷)
　　等级：心灵术士3
　　异象：视觉
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：接触
　　目标：接触到的生物
　　持续时间：10分钟/等级
　　豁免检定：无
　　异能抗力：可 (无害)
　　灵能点：5
　　受术者可以以90呎的速度飞行（如果穿着中甲或重甲则只能以60呎速度飞行）。他能以一半速度上升，或者以两倍速度下降。他的机动性为良好。使用“飞行术”飞行的专注程度与行走相同，所以受术者可以正常攻击或显能。受术者可以冲刺但不能奔跑。物品加上防具负重不能超过最大负重限制（参见《玩家手册》第九章）。
　　超过持续时间後，若受术者仍在空中，灵能效果会渐渐消失。受术者会先以60呎/轮的速度下降1d6轮。如果在这段时间内落地则算平安降落。如果没有落地，剩下高度就会自由落下。（每10呎造成1d6摔伤）。如果“飞行术”被某种消解灵能的方式所解除，受术者将立即跌落{Andor注:这点和对应法术不同}。
　
Fly
　　Psychoportation (Dex)
　　Level: Psion 3
　　Display: Vi
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Touch
　　Target: Creature touched
　　Duration: 10 minutes/level
　　Saving Throw: None
　　Power Resistance: Yes (harmless)
　　Power Points: 5
　　The power’s subject can fly at a speed of 90 feet (60 feet if the creature wears medium or heavy armor). The subject can fly up at half speed and descend at double speed. The flying subject’s maneuverability is good. Using the fly power requires as much concentration as walking, so the subject can attack or manifest powers normally. The subject of fly can charge but not run, and it cannot carry aloft more weight than its maximum load (see Chapter 9 of the Player’s Handbook), plus any armor it wears.
　　Should the duration expire while the subject is still aloft, the psionic effect fails slowly. The subject drops 60 feet per round for 1d6 rounds. If it reaches the ground in this time, it is safe. If not, it falls the rest of the distance (falling damage is 1d6 per 10 feet of fall). Since negating a power effectively ends it, the subject falls immediately if fly is negated.
　
强制连心术(Forced Mindlink)
　　心灵附魔系(魅力)
　　等级：心灵术士4
　　豁免检定：意志，通过则无效
　　异能抗力：可
　　灵能点：7
　　如同“次级连心术”，但你可以尝试与不情愿的生物建立心灵链接。不过即使你成功的与不情愿的生物建立了心灵链接，它也可以选择不通过心灵链接对你“说话”。
　
Forced Mindlink
　　Telepathy (Cha)
　　Level: Psion 4
　　Saving Throw: Will negates
　　Power Resistance: Yes
　　Power Points: 7
　　As lesser mindlink, except you can attempt to create a telepathic bond with a creature who is not willing. Even if you create a bond with an unwilling creature, it can still decide not to “speak” with you through the telepathic bond.
　
预警术(Foresight)
　　心灵预言系(感知)
　　等级：心灵术士8
　　异象：视觉、物质
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：个人 或 接触
　　目标：见说明
　　持续时间：10分钟/等级
　　豁免检定：无 或 意志，通过则无效 (见说明)
　　异能抗力：不可 或 可 (无害)
　　灵能点：15
　　你获得关于自己或他人的强大第六感。当异能展现後，只要有人想危害受术者，你就会立刻警觉。因此，如果本异能目标是自己，如果有游荡者企图偷袭你，或有生物将从你没注意到的方向跳出，或有攻击者正用异能、法术或远程武器瞄准你，你都会事先警觉。你不会被突袭或措手不及。此外，本异能可使你以直觉行动保护自己——弯腰、向右跳、闭眼等等——并给你的AC和反射豁免以+2洞察加值。只要你失去对AC的敏捷加值，你就失去该洞察加值。
　　当异能目标是其他生物时，你会得到关于该生物的警报。你必须与之沟通才能传达有用的警报，否则他可能在无预警的情况下中埋伏。你得到警报後，及时大声警告、将他拉住，甚至心灵感应沟通（通过相应异能），都可以达到预警目的。然而，目标并无AC或反射检定的洞察加值。
　
Foresight
　　Clairsentience (Wis)
　　Level: Psion 8
　　Display: Vi, Ma
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Personal or touch
　　Target: See text
　　Duration: 10 minutes/level
　　Saving Throw: None or Will negates (see text)
　　Power Resistance: No or Yes (harmless)
　　Power Points: 15
　　You gain a powerful sixth sense in relation to yourself or another. Once the power is manifested, you receive instantaneous warnings of impending danger or harm to the subject of the power. Thus, if you are the subject of the power you would be warned in advance if a rogue were about to attempt a sneak attack on you, or if a creature were about to leap out from an unexpected direction, or if an attacker were specifically targeting you with a power, spell, or ranged weapon. You are never surprised or flat-footed. In addition, the power gives you a general idea of what action you might take to best protect yourself—duck, jump right, close your eyes, and so on—and gives you a +2 insight bonus to AC and Reflex saves. This insight bonus is lost whenever you would lose a Dexterity bonus to AC.
　　When another creature is the object of the power, you receive warnings about that creature. You must communicate what you learn to the other creature for the warning to be useful, and it can be caught unprepared in the absence of such a warning. Shouting a warning, yanking a person back, and even telepathically communicating (via an appropriate power) can all be accomplished before some danger befalls me power’s subject, provided you act on the warning without delay. The subject, however, does not gain the insight bonus to AC or Reflex saves.
　
行动自如(Freedom of Movement)
　　心灵传送系(敏捷)
　　等级：心灵术士4
　　异象：听觉
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：个人 或 接触
　　目标：自己 或 接触到的生物
　　持续时间：10分钟/等级
　　豁免检定：无
　　异能抗力：不可 或 可 (无害)
　　灵能点：7
　　即使处于妨碍行动的灵能（例如“星质茧”）的影响下，你或你碰触的受术者仍然能正常移动和攻击。
　　本异能同样可以令受术者在水中如常移动和攻击，只要武器是手持挥舞而非投掷的，无论是挥砍武器（比如斧和剑）或钝击武器（比如链枷、战锤和硬头锤）都可如常进行攻击。但是，本异能并不能使受术者得到水下呼吸的能力，受术者需要从其他的灵能中得到这个能力。
　
Freedom of Movement
　　Psychoportation (Dex)
　　Level: Psion 4
　　Display: Au
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Personal or touch
　　Target: You or creature touched
　　Duration: 10 minutes/level
　　Saving Throw: None
　　Power Resistance: No or Yes (harmless)
　　Power Points: 7
　　This power enables you or the creature you touch to move and attack normally for the duration of the power, even under the influence of psionics that usually impedes movement, such as ectoplasmic cocoon.
　　The power also allows a character to move and attack normally while underwater, even with cutting weapons such as axes and swords and with smashing weapons such as flails, hammers, and maces, provided that the weapon is wielded in the hand rather than hurled. The power does not, however, allow water breathing without further appropriate psionic adaptation.
