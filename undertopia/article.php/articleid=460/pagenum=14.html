Great Dismal Library

异能详述-Q、R、S字头系列

 萃取术(Quintessence)
　　心灵创造系(智力)
　　等级：心灵术士4
　　异象：物质 (见说明)
　　显能时间：1轮
　　距离：近距 (25+5/2等级)
　　效果：1盎司“精粹” (见说明)
　　持续时间：立即
　　豁免检定：无
　　异能抗力：不可
　　灵能点：7
　　你能够瓦解连续时间中的一小块，形成一团1贾本兜呐ǔ淼慕鹤次铮这被称为“精粹”。从某些角度观察，“精粹”如同银镜一样反射微光，而从另一些角度观测则是完全透明的。它接触到的皮肤首先会感到冰冷的震动而後慢慢转为完全的麻木。你可以把一小团“精粹”涂在任何小物品的表面，例如一把钥匙，一枚戒指，一张封条或者一枚勋章。
　　被封闭于精粹之中的物品不受时间流逝的影响；用术语来讲，它们进入了一个停滞的空间。仅有部分接触到“精粹”的活物肉体也将部分的从时光流动中拉出来。这种分裂将在部分接触开始的10轮後导致每轮1点伤害。
　　“精粹”可以被手工从受保护物体上擦去，将它重新投入时间流动中去。抹去保护膜有75%的机会使“精粹”蒸发并重新回到连续时间中。或者，它将重新结合成一个一贾本兜乃珠形，可以被再度使用。
　　大量的“精粹”集中起来理论上可以保存大型物品或建筑物（或甚至一个完整的活物，如此它将不会受到部分接触造成的伤害）。不过，灵能人物和生物一般不愿意这样做，因为一磅以上的“精粹”团会产生一半强度的“抑能术”效果（DC=16，影响半径5眨包括显能者在内的所有人都将受到影响）。
　
Quintessence
　　Metacreativity (Int)
　　Level: Psion 4
　　Display: Ma (see text)
　　Manifestation Time: 1 round
　　Range: Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
　　Effect: 1 ounce dollop of quintessence (see text)
　　Duration: Instantaneous
　　Saving Throw: None
　　Power Resistance: No
　　Power Points: 7
　　You can collapse a bit of time from the continuum, forming a 1-inch-diameter dollop of thick, gooey material called quintessence. Quintessence shimmers like a silver mirror from some angles but is transparent from others. It brings a cool shock that slowly fades to complete numbness after contact with the skin. You can smooth a dollop of quintessence around any small object, such as a key, a ring, a seal, or an insignia.
　　Objects sealed within quintessence are protected from the effects of time; in practical terms, they enter a state of stasis. Living flesh with only partial contact with quintessence is also partially pulled out of the timestream. This disruption deals 1 point of damage per round beginning 10 rounds after partial contact begins.
　　Quintessence can be manually scraped away from a protected object, freeing it to rejoin the time stream. Removing the protective film is 75% likely to force the quintessence to evaporate back into the continuum. Otherwise, it coalesces again into a 1-inch-diameter bead, available for later usage.
　　Large quantities of quintessence could theoretically be gathered to preserve large items or structures (or even a complete living creature, which would prevent the damage taken from partial contact). However, psionic characters and creatures are generally loath to do so because accumulations of quintessence weighing 1 pound or more generate a halfstrength catapsi effect (DC 16, radius 5 feet, all are affected, including caster).

 体验剧痛(Recall Agony)
　　心灵预言系(感知)
　　等级：心灵术士5
　　灵能点：9
　　如同“体验痛苦”，但折叠的时间展示出的伤害更严重：对手遭受9d6点伤害。
　
Recall Agony
　　Clairsentience (Wis)
　　Level: Psion 5
　　Power Points: 9
　　As recall pain, except the wounds revealed by folding the fourth dimension are worse: The foe takes 9d6 points of damage.
　
体验死亡(Recall Death)
　　心灵预言系(感知)[死亡]
　　等级：心灵术士8
　　距离：近距 (25+5/2等级)
　　豁免检定：意志，通过则部分生效
　　灵能点：15
　　如同“体验痛苦”，但折叠的时间展示出的伤害是致命的。目标可以进行意志豁免以尝试在此攻击下生还。如果豁免成功，他将只受到3d6+15点伤害。
　
Recall Death
　　Clairsentience (Wis) [Death]
　　Level: Psion 8
　　Range: Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
　　Saving Throw: Will partial
　　Power Points: 15
　　As recall pain, except the wounds revealed by folding the fourth dimension are mortal. The target is entitled to a Will save to survive the attack. If she succeeds, she instead sustains 3d6+15 points of damage.
　
体验痛苦(Recall Pain)
　　心灵预言系(感知)
　　等级：心灵术士2
　　异象：物质
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：中距 (100+10/等级)
　　目标：单一活物
　　持续时间：立即
　　豁免检定：意志，通过则效果减半
　　异能抗力：可
　　灵能点：3
　　时间的构造按你的意愿断裂，展示出对手曾经（或将会）受到的创伤。该对手由于过去（或未来）对现在的暂时冲击而遭受3d6点的伤害。
　
Recall Pain
　　Clairsentience (Wis)
　　Level: Psion 2
　　Display: Ma
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
　　Target: One living creature
　　Duration: Instantaneous
　　Saving Throw: Will half
　　Power Resistance: Yes
　　Power Points: 3
　　The fabric of time parts to your will, revealing wounds your foe has received in the past (or has yet to receive). That foe takes 3d6 points of damage as the past (or future) impinges briefly on the present.
　
异能回弹(Reddopsi)
　　心灵转化系(体质)
　　等级：心灵术士7
　　异象：听觉、嗅觉、精神
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：个人
　　目标：自己
　　持续时间：直到耗尽或10分钟/等级
　　灵能点：13
　　当你展现“异能回弹”时，以你为目标的心灵异能（不包括攻击模式）将弹回给原先的显能者。本异能只回弹以你为目标的异能。效果型和区域型异能不受影响。“异能回弹”也不会回应距离为“接触”的异能。
　　“异能回弹”可以反转任何异能，但反转後便结束，不管被反转的异能多么弱。如果你将某异能反转给了也受到“异能反弹”影响的显能者，那么该异能再次回弹给你。
　
Reddopsi
　　Psychokinesis (Con)
　　Level: Psion 7
　　Display: Au, Ol, Me
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Personal
　　Target: You
　　Duration: Until expended or 10 minutes/level
　　Power Points: 13
　　When you manifest reddopsi [red-ob-sigh], psionic powers (excluding attack modes) targeted against you rebound on the original manifester. This reverses only powers that have you as a target. Area powers and those that target effects are not affected. Reddopsi also fails to reflect touch range powers.
　　Reddopsi can reverse any power, but ends as soon as it has done so, no matter how minor the power. Should you reverse a power back on a manifester who also is affected by reddopsi, the power rebounds once more upon you.
　
回春术(Rejuvenation)
　　心灵自塑系(力量)
　　等级：心灵术士3
　　异象：听觉
　　显能时间：1分钟
　　距离：个人
　　目标：自己
　　持续时间：10小时
　　灵能点：5
　　“回春术”每小时都可以治疗1点暂时属性伤害，直到治愈或治疗10点为止。本异能并不能恢复永久性属性吸取（比如缚灵的碰触造成的属性伤害）。
　
Rejuvenation
　　Psychometabolism (Str)
　　Level: Psion 3
　　Display: Au
　　Manifestation Time: 1 minute
　　Range: Personal
　　Target: You
　　Duration: 10 hours
　　Power Points: 5
　　Rejuvenation cures 1 point of temporary ability damage per hour, up to a maximum of 10 points. It does not restore permanent ability drain (such as from a wraith’s touch).
　
遥视陷阱(Remote View Trap)
　　心灵预言系(感知)
　　等级：心灵术士6
　　异象：视觉、精神
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：个人
　　目标：自己
　　持续时间：24小时 + 1小时/等级
　　豁免检定：无
　　异能抗力：不可
　　灵能点：11
　　当他人使用“锐耳术/鹰眼术”、“遥视术”或其他从远处观察你的方法时，你准备好的陷阱将给他们一个可怕的意外。在尝试从远处观察你之前，遥视者和你立即进行“遥视”对抗检定，但你的检定获得+10洞察加值。（没有“遥视”技能的生物以智力检定代替。如果对抗魔法探知，则以“遥视”检定对抗对方的“探知”检定。）如果你的结果不低于遥视者，则你未被发现。此外，尝试遥视者从这次麻烦中受到4d4点生物电流（电击）伤害。你意识到有人尝试观察你，但不知道对方的身份及位置。
　
Remote View Trap
　　Clairsentience (Wis)
　　Level: Psion 6
　　Display: Vi, Me
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Personal
　　Target: You
　　Duration: 24 hours + 1 hour/level
　　Saving Throw: None
　　Power Resistance: No
　　Power Points: 11
　　When others use clairaudience/clairvoyance, remote viewing, or other means of observing you from afar, your prepared trap gives them a nasty surprise. Prior to an attempt to view you from afar, you and the remote viewer immediately make opposed Remote View checks, but you gain a +10 insight bonus on your check. (A Remote View check is the same as an Intelligence check for a creature without the Remote View skill. If opposing a magical scrying, your Remote View check is opposed by the opponent’s Scry check.) If you meet or beat the remote viewer’s result, you are undetected. Moreover, the would-be observer takes 4d14 points of bioelectrical (electricity), damage for his trouble. You are aware of the attempt to view you, but not of the perpetrator or the perpetrator’s location.
　
遥视术(Remote Viewing)
　　心灵预言系(感知)
　　等级：心灵术士3
　　异象：听觉、视觉 (见说明)
　　显能时间：1小时
　　距离：见说明
　　效果：灵能感应场
　　持续时间：1分钟/等级
　　豁免检定：无
　　异能抗力：不可
　　灵能点：5
　　你能看见和听见任意距离外的生物，但必须通过“遥视”检定才行。难度取决于你对目标的了解程度以及你拥有（如果拥有的话）的关于目标的实体线索的类型。此外，若目标在其他位面，你的“遥视”检定得到-5减值。

Remote Viewing

　　Level: Psion 3
　　Display: Au, Vi (see text)
　　Manifestation Time: 1 hour
　　Range: See text
　　Effect: Psionic sensor
　　Duration: 1 minute/level
　　Saving Throw: None
　　Power Resistance: No
　　Power Points: 5
　　You can see and hear some creature, who may be at any distance. You must succeed at a Remote View check to do so. The difficulty of the task depends on how well you know the subject and what sort of physical connection (if any) you have to that creature. Furthermore, if the subject is on another plane, you get a -5 penalty on the Remote View check.

　　Missive and darkvision can be manifested through remote viewing. Aura sight can be manifested through remote viewing but with only a 5% chance per manifester level of operating correctly.
　
回收术(Retrieve)
　　心灵传送系(敏捷)[传送]
　　等级：心灵术士6
　　异象：视觉
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：中距 (100+10/等级)
　　目标：一个你可以用手抓住的，重量不超过10磅/等级的物体
　　持续时间：立即
　　豁免检定：无 (见说明)
　　异能抗力：不可
　　灵能点：11
　　你可以把异能有效距离内你可以看到的物体直接传送到你的手里。如果该物体正被对手占有或持用，你需要尝试以远程攻击加值来卸除武器，如同你正在使用与对手所用相同尺寸的武器。这不会导致借机攻击(参见《玩家手册》第八章中的“卸除武器”)。此时你的攻击检定得到+12能力加值。如果你成功，则你可以“回收”该物品。
　
Retrieve
　　Psychoportation (Dex) [Teleportation]
　　Level: Psion 6
　　Display: Vi
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
　　Target: One object you could hold in one hand, weighing up to 10 lb./level
　　Duration: Instantaneous
　　Saving Throw: None (see text)
　　Power Resistance: No
　　Power Points: 11
　　You can teleport an item you can see within range directly to your hand. If the object is in the possession of or held by an opponent, you make a disarm attempt using a ranged attack, as if with a weapon the same size as your opponent's. This does not draw an attack of opportunity (see Disarm in Chapter 8 of the Player's Handbook). You gain a +12 competence bonus on your attack roll. If you win, you successfully retrieve the contended object.
　
心智分裂(Schism)
　　心灵附魔系(魅力)
　　等级：心灵术士3
　　异象：视觉、听觉
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：个人
　　目标：自己
　　持续时间：1轮/等级 (解消)
　　灵能点：5
　　你的心智分裂成两个独立的部分。每个部分都完全自主的行动，就好像一个身体中有两个人格一样。新分裂出来的心智无法控制身体，但是可以每轮进行一个纯思维的部分动作（比如展现异能）。两个心智可以以传心术互相沟通。两个都可以使用心灵异能，甚至可以同时使用，虽然双方的灵能点都来自同一个储备。由新分裂出的心智展现的异能将比标准费用多花费2点。
　　这个效果使你每轮可以多做一个部分动作，你可以在标准动作之前或之後做出，只要这个动作是展现异能或其他纯精神活动。在威胁区域内展现异能仍然会引起借机攻击。
　　如果你在分裂期间受到暗示，你可以在第一次豁免检定失败之後再做一次豁免检定。如果你两次豁免均失败，那么你结束分裂状态并且如常受到这个异能的影响。如果你有一次未通过，另外一个心智仍然可以正常行动。
　　注意：法术“加速术”无法作用于“精神分裂”的生物，这是由于它的片断状思维。其他DM所指定的法术与异能同样未必能够生效，或者在最好的情况下，能影响到一个人格。
　
Schism
　　Telepathy (Cha)
　　Level: Psion 3
　　Display: Vi, Au
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Personal
　　Target: You
　　Duration: 1 round/level (D)
　　Power Points: 5
　　Your mind splits into two independent parts. Each part functions in complete autonomy, like two characters in one body. The newly partitioned mind does not control the body physically but is free to take one partial action each round that is completely mental (such as manifesting a power). Both minds communicate with each other telepathically. Both can use psionic powers, even at the same time, although both draw from the same power point pool. All powers manifested by the newly partitioned personality cost a number of power points equal to their standard cost + 2.
　　This effect allows you to take an extra partial action each round, either before or after your regular action, as long as that action is the manifestation of a power or some other nonphysical activity. Manifesting powers in threatened areas still provokes attacks of opportunity.
　　If you are subjected to a compulsion or charm effect while you are of two minds, make a second saving throw if you fail the first. If you fail both, then schism ends and you are affected normally by the power. If you fail one, the other part of your mind is still free to act normally.
　　 Note: The spell haste does not function on a “split-minded” creature due to its fragmented consciousness. Other spells and powers designated by the DM are likewise unlikely to function, or at the very best, to affect only one of the personalities.
　
识破隐形(See Invisibility)
　　心灵预言系(感知)
　　等级：心灵术士2
　　异象：视觉
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：中距 (100+10/等级)
　　区域：锥状
　　持续时间：10分钟/等级 (解消)
　　豁免检定：无
　　异能抗力：不可
　　灵能点：3
　　你能看见任何隐形的物体或生物，还有星界体和以太体，如同他们是正常可见的。
　　本异能无法揭示隐形的方法，不过有银线的星界旅行者很容易识别。本异能无法揭示幻术或让你看穿不透明的物体。本异能无法揭示躲藏、隐蔽或其他难以发现的生物。
　
See Invisibility
　　Clairsentience (Wis)
　　Level: Psion 2
　　Display: Vi
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
　　Area: Cone
　　Duration: 10 minutes/level (D)
　　Saving Throw: None
　　Power Resistance: No
　　Power Points: 3
　　You see any objects or beings that are invisible, as well as any that are astral or ethereal, as if they were normally visible.
　　The power does not reveal the method used to obtain invisibility, although an astral traveler is easy to identify if he has a silver cord. It does not reveal illusions or enable you to see through opaque objects. It does not reveal creatures that are simply hiding, concealed, or otherwise hard to see.
　
观声术(See Sound)
　　心灵自塑系(力量)
　　等级：心灵术士1、心灵武士1
　　异象：视觉
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：个人
　　目标：自己

滑行术(Skate)
　　心灵传送系(敏捷)
　　等级：心灵术士1、心灵武士1
　　异象：视觉、物质
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：接触
　　目标：自己 或 自愿的单一生物 或 单一物品 (总重量至多100磅/等级)
　　持续时间：1分钟/等级 (解消)
　　豁免检定：无
　　异能抗力：不可
　　灵能点：1
　　你、另一个自愿生物或一个无人持有的物品可以在固体地面上滑行，犹如在光滑的冰面上滑行一般。如果“滑行术”被展现于自己或自愿生物，受术者仍旧依照他的意愿保持平衡，这使他可以优雅的在地面上滑行、转弯或急停。在持续时间内，受术者的基本速度增加15眨受术者可以在向上或向下倾斜的斜面上滑行，前提是该斜面是他平时可以安全行走的。上坡将使滑行者的速度降低到正常水平，而下坡将使滑行者的基本速度+30而不是+15。这些加值被视为增强加值。
　　如果本异能被展现于物体，则在沿地面拖动目标物体时它视为仅有原有重量的1/10。

暗示(Suggestion)
　　心灵附魔系(魅力)[胁迫、影响心灵、依赖语言]
　　等级：心灵术士2
　　异象：听觉
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：近距 (25+5/2等级)
　　目标：单一活物
　　持续时间：1小时/等级 或 直到完成
　　豁免检定：意志，通过则无效
　　异能抗力：可
　　灵能点：3
　　你对受术者进行暗示（最多两句话）来影响他的行为。暗示的词句必须听起来合理。暗示生物自残、跳上矛尖、自杀或者做明显有害的事情将会自动使本异能失效。不过，暗示一池强酸是纯净的水，浸泡一下可以清除疲劳则另当别论。劝告红龙停止攻击你的队伍，以利于双方共同寻宝同样属于本异能的合理使用范围。
　　暗示的行动可以维持整个持续时间，例如在上面提到的红龙的情况下。如果暗示的行动可以在很短的时间内完成，本异能将在受术者完成他被要求做的事情後结束。你可以设定特殊条件来触发特定动作。比如，你可以暗示一位贵族骑士把他的战马送给他遇到的第一个乞丐。如果他在异能结束之前都没有遇到这种情况，则动作将不会做出。非常合理的暗示可以使受术者的豁免检定受到一定的减值（比如-1、-2等），由DM判断。
　
Suggestion
　　Telepathy (Cha) [Compulsion, Mind-Affecting, Language-Dependent]
　　Level: Psion 2
　　Display: Au
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
　　Target: One living creature
　　Duration: 1 hour/level or until completed
　　Saving Throw: Will negates
　　Power Resistance: Yes
　　Power Points: 3
　　You influence the actions of the subject creature by suggesting a course of action (limited to a sentence or two). The suggestion must be worded in such a manner as to make the action sound reasonable. Asking the creature to stab itself, throw itself onto a spear, immolate itself, or do some other obviously harmful act automatically negates the effect of the power. However, a suggestion that a pool of acid is actually pure water and that a quick dip would be refreshing is another matter. Urging a red dragon to stop attacking your party so that the dragon and party could jointly loot a rich treasure elsewhere is likewise a reasonable use of the power.
　　The suggested course of action can continue for the entire duration, such as in the case of the red dragon mentioned above. If the suggested action can be completed in a shorter time, the power ends when the subject finishes what he was asked to do. You can instead specify conditions that will trigger a special action during the duration. For example, you might suggest that a noble knight give her warhorse to the first beggar she meets. If the condition is not met before the power expires, the action is not performed. A very reasonable suggestion causes the saving throw to be made with a penalty (such as -1, -2, and so on) at the discretion of the DM.
　
暂缓生命(Suspend Life)
　　心灵自塑系(力量)
　　等级：心灵术士6、心灵武士6
　　异象：嗅觉
　　显能时间：单动作
　　距离：个人
　　目标：自己
　　持续时间：立即
　　灵能点：11
　　你让自己陷入极其深沉的出神状态以至于你的生命活动近于停止。即使是侦测生命力或思想的异能也无法察觉你还活着。当你处于出神状态，一年的流逝对你来说不过是过了一天。尽管你的生物钟变慢了，你仍然会在一“天”（虽然实际上已经过了一年）的不吃不喝之後开始饿并因此而感觉到正常的饥饿感与口渴感。你仍然可以感知你的环境，虽然完成于10分钟以内的事件对你来说发生的太快了以至于无法察觉。如果你受到伤害，你将在4轮内从出神状态中恢复。如果你自愿从出神状态恢复，则需要10回合。一旦你结束了这次出神，你必须再次展现本异能来回到暂缓状态。
　
Suspend Life
　　Psychometabolism (Str)
　　Level: Psion 6/Psychic Warrior 6
　　Display: Ol
　　Manifestation Time: 1 action
　　Range: Personal
　　Target: You
　　Duration: Instantaneous
　　Power Points: 11
　　You can place yourself into a trance so deep that all your life functions are essentially halted. Even powers that detect life or thought are incapable of determining that you yet live. While you are suspended, you feel the passage of one day for every year that actually passes. Though on a slower schedule, you grow hungry after a “day” without food (though a year may pass in actuality) and begin to suffer the effects of thirst and starvation as appropriate. You are also aware of your surroundings, though events that take less time than 10 minutes occur too quickly for you to note them. If you take damage, you come out of your trance in 4 rounds. If you choose to come out of the trance voluntarily, it takes 10 rounds. Once you leave the trance, you must manifest this power once more to return to a state of suspension.
