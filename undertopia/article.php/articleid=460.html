心灵术士的异能

 第五章·异能详述
POWERS --- CHAPTER FIVE
　
{ Andor注：以下由 Jim Raynor 翻译，Andor 审校。 }
　
　　本章的前半部将会依心灵术士和心灵武士的职业列出异能列表。之後则依异能名称排序，列出所有异能的详细说明。{Jim Raynor注：原文在列表中使用缩写，译文未使用，故未翻译缩写表。}
　　This chapter begins with the power lists of the psion and psychic warrior classes. The psion power list uses abbreviated names as follows: Clasen (Clairsentience); Metcre (Metacreativity); Psykin (Psychokinesis); Psymet (Psychometabolism); Psyport (Psychoportation); and Telep (Telepathy). The rest of the chapter contains power descriptions in alphabetical order by power name.
　
　　异能链：有些异能需要参考它们所根源的基本异能。在异能链中排序较後的异能，只说明其与基本异能不同的部分。与基本异能相同的项目或其他描述则不再重复。
　　Power Chains: Some powers reference other powers that they are based upon. Only information in a power later in the power chain the is different from the base power is covered in the power being described. Header entries and other information that are the same as the base power are not repeated.
　
　　生命骰：在异能效果上，“生命骰”就等于是“人物等级”。（生物的生命骰只与种族有关，无关职业。生物的人物等级即等于其生命骰数。）
　　Hit Dice: The term “Hit Dice” is used synonymously with “character levels” for effects that a number of Hit Dice of creatures. (Creatures with only Hit Dice, not classes, have character levels equal to their Hit Dice.)
　
　　显能者等级：异能效果和持续时间通常取决于显能者等级，也就是显能者的职业等级。若该生物没有职业，则其显能者等级等于其生命骰数，除非另有说明。
　　Manifester Level: A power’s effect and duration often depends on manifester level, which is the manifester’s psionic class level. Creatures with no classes have a manifester level equal to their Hit Dice unless otherwise specified.
　
　　生物与人物：在异能说明中，“生物”与“人物”同义。
　　Creatures and Characters: “Creatures” and “characters” are used synonymously in these power descriptions.
　
　　类似法术的异能：与《玩家手册》中的法术同名的异能具有和该法术相同的效果。区别是心灵异能是类法术能力。
　　Powers that Look like Spells: A power with the same name as a spell in the Player’s Handbook has the same effect as that spell. The difference is that psionic powers are spell-like abilities.

0-LEVEL PSION POWERS (Talents)
　
心灵预言系
Clasen

　　Detect Poison. {Andor: absence in original text.}

　　Know Direction. You know which way is north.
　
心灵创造系
Metcre

　　Bolt. You create a short-lived bolt, arrow, or bullet.

　　Finger of Fire. You deal 1d3 fire damage to one foe.
　
　　创造杂物：创造一个仅能持续很短时间的小物件。
　　Trinket. You create a short-lived trinket.
　
心灵转化系
Psykin
　
　　操控阴影：你能像操纵木偶一般的操纵一个普通的影子。
　　Control Shadow. You control a normal shadow like a puppet.
　
　　隔空之手：低级的“心灵遥控”。
　　Far Hand. Minor telekinesis.
　
　　隔空之击：念动力的冲击造成1点伤害。
　　Far Punch. Telekinetic punch deals 1 damage.
　
　　照明术：你的眼睛射出20呎的光锥。
　　My Light. Your eyes emit a 20-ft. cone of light.
　
心灵自塑系
Psymet
　
　　精灵视觉：你获得昏暗视觉。
　　Elfsight. You have low-light vision.
　
　　次级天生护甲：你得到+1天生防御加值。
　　Lesser Natural Armor. You gain +1 natural armor bonus.
　
　　魔爪术：你的徒手攻击造成+1点额外伤害。
　　Talons. Your unarmed attacks deal +1 damage.
　
　　魄力术：你得到1点暂时生命值。
　　Verve. You gain 1 temporary hit point.
　
心灵传送系
Psyport
　
　　速度爆发：目标速度提高10呎，持续一轮。
　　Burst. Subject speed improves by 10 ft. for 1 round.
　
　　猫落术：你在跌落时能适当的平衡身体。
　　Catfall. You recover well from a fall.
　
　　漂浮术：使一个物体漂浮在水或其他液体上。
　　Float. You buoy a subject in water or other liquid.
　
心灵附魔系
Telep
　
　　晕眩术：生物失去下一次的动作。
　　Daze. Creature loses next action.
　
　　分神术：目标神智不清，在做某些动作时受到-1减值。
　　Distract. Subject’s mind wanders, imparting a -1 penalty on some actions.
　
　　心灵短讯：向目标送出一条单向的心灵短讯。
　　Missive. You send a one-way telepathic message to subject.
　
　　心灵投影：更改目标的情绪。
　　Telempathic Projection. You modify subject’s emotions.
