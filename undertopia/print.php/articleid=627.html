地区专长
Regional feats


占星[一般专长]
占星术士在其所在城市的社会中扮演着重要的角色。贵族家庭常常雇用他们来确定收割日程或其他事宜，而商队主雇佣他们来导航。
地区：Draj、Nibenay
优势：你的知识(占星)技能获得+2加值，并在能够看到太阳、月亮或星星的情况下避免迷路的生存技能获得+4加值。{survival？然则这本书难道不是3e的么}
Astrologer [General]
Astrologers play an important role in the societies of their cities. Noble families often employ their expertise to plan harvests and other projects, while caravan masters hire their services as navigators.
Regions: Draj, Nibenay
Benefit: You gain a +2 bonus to Knowledge (astronomy) checks and +4 bonus to Survival checks made to avoid getting lost when able to see the sun, moon or stars.

强力灵能之拳[一般专长]{口胡，难道不是灵能专长么}
Draj的心灵术士们花费了数个世纪来掌握身心合一的境界并完善了灵能之拳这项技巧。
地区：Draj
先决条件：灵能之拳
优势：如同灵能之拳(参见《心灵异能手册》)，不过支付2点灵能点来造成2d4额外伤害。

Greater Psionic Fist [General]
The Draji psions have used centuries to master the union of body and mind and perfected the Psionic Fist technique.
Region: Draj
Prerequisites: Psionic Fist
Benefit: As Psionic Fist (see Psionics Handbook), but pay 2 power points to inflict 2d4 extra points of damage.


强力灵能武器[一般专长]{同上}
心灵自塑能力和心灵转化能力指导下的Draj的心灵术士完善了灵能武器这项技巧。
地区：Draj
先决条件：猛力攻击、灵能武器
优势：如同灵能之拳(参见《心灵异能手册》)，不过支付2点灵能点来造成2d4额外伤害。

Greater Psionic Weapon [General]
The psychometabolism and psychokinesis oriented Draji psions have perfected the Psionic Weapon technique.
Region: Draj
Prerequisites: Power Attack, Psionic Weapon
Benefit: As Psionic Weapon (see Psionics Handbook), but pay 2 power points to inflict 2d4 extra points of damage.

美洲虎之吼[一般专长]
你能引导你的力量发出恐怖的啸声击溃敌人的士气。
地区：Draj
先决条件：威吓 9级，魅力13或以上
优势，每回合一次，你能够以一个自由动作使用美洲虎之吼。所有30尺内并能够听到你的吼声的智慧生物都必须通过意志豁免(DC 10+等级+魅力调整值)，否则将战栗(攻击、豁免和技能检定都受到-2士气减值)2d4回合。成功的豁免表示该生物在一日内不会再受到美洲虎之吼的影响。

Jaguar Roar [General]
You can channel your strength and emit a terrifying roar that demoralizes enemies.
Region: Draj
Prerequisites: Intimidate 9 ranks, Charisma 13+
Benefit: You can, as a free action once per round, use the Jaguar Roar. All intelligent creatures within 30 feet who can hear the roar must roll a Will save (DC 10 + level + Charisma modifier) or become shaken (-2 morale penalty to attack rolls, saves and skill checks) for 2d4 rounds. A successful save means an opponent cannot be affected by the Jaguar Roar that day.


固执[一般专长]
该处人民是有名的顽固民族，他们的顽固和坏脾气是出了名的。
地区：Draj、Mul
优势：你的意志豁免获得+1加值，威吓技能检定获得+2加值

Mekillothead [General]
Your people are an obstinate folk known for stubbornness and ill temperedness.
Regions: Draj, Mul
Benefit: You gain a +1 bonus to Will saves and a +2 bonus to Intimidate checks.
